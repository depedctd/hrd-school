INSET SY 2025-2026

Conduct of School-Based In-Service Training for School Year 2025-2026

October 27-30, 2025

School-based Training


Objectives

▪ To strengthen teachers' knowledge of subject content;

▪ To improve teaching methods to better meet the needs of students.


Target Participants:

All teachers in service


No. of Target Participants No. of Actual Participants Percentage of Actual VS Target
3,652


Key Training Area Focus


▪ Relative practice and instructional refinement in the implementation of the
a. Revised K-12 Curriculum
b. ARAL-Reading Program

▪ Strenthening inclusive instruction

▪ Strategies for promoting literacy and numeracy

▪ Strategies for developing critical and creative thinking as well as other higher order thinking skills

▪ Instructional strategies for non-major teachers handling Filipino in Junior and Senior High School

▪ Empowering teachers on Socio-Emotional Learning (SEL)in classroom instruction

▪ Strategies for supporting continuity of learning during emergency situations through flexible modalities

▪ Accelerating digital adoption for effective and accessible learning delivery

▪ Strenthening instruction and assessment in the Alternative Learning System

▪ Enhanced delivery of Guidance services by Guidance designates in schools

▪ Other emerging needs based on teachers' individual professional development plan
