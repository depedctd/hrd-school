Exit Con on INSET and LAC

Exit Conference on the Implementation of In-Service Training and Learning Action Cell SY 2025-2026

March 23 (Bagamanoc, Caramoran, San Andres, Secondary), March 24 (Baras, Gigmoto, Viga, Secondary)
March 25 (Bato, Virac, Secondary), March 26 (Pandan, Panganiban, San Miguel, Secondary)

Venue: Rhaj Executive Inn, Gogon, Virac


Objectives

▪ Identify strengths and areas for improvement during the conduct of the activity.

▪ Document insights and recommendations based on the outcomes of the activity,
ensuring the submission of a consolidated report per District

▪ Strengthen collaboration and engage in continuous professional development among teaching and non-teaching personnel.

▪ Prepare and submit documentary requeirements for School Professional Development Program
and ensuring compliance with NEAP standards


Target Participants:

School Heads or any member of SPDC
