Design, Development, and QA of Master Teacher Module

Division Training Workshop on the Design, Development, and Quality Assurance of Master Teacher Module

November 24, 25-26, December 1-4, 8-13, 15-16, 2025

Venue: Amenia Beach Resort, Palawig, San Andres


Dates Activities
November 24, 2025 Division Staff Orientation Workshop
November 25-26, 2025 Writeshop of Session Guides and Slide Decks
December 1-4, 2025 Quality Assurance of SGs and Slides
December 8-9, 2025 Writeshop of Draft Modules
December 10-11, 2025 Pilot Testing of the Modules
December 12-13, 2025 Revision of the Modules
December 15-16, 2025 Finalization of the Modules

Objectives

▪ Develop the module for Master Teachers On-boarding Program as output from the session guide writing workshop

▪ Draft a professional development module aligned to identify competency needs using the DepEd L&D template,
and guided by the RPMS-PPST Framework

▪ Apply quality assurance criteria to evaluate the draft of the PD Program for Master Teachers
