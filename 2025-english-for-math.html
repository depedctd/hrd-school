English for Math

English for Mathematics: Enhancing Language and Skills for Math Learners


September 26-28, 2025

Rhaj Executive Inn, Gogon, Virac, Catanduanes


Objectives

The activity aims to enhance teachers' capacity to use the English language
effectively in the teaching of Mathematics to improve learners' comprehension and
numeracy performance. Specifically, it seeks to:

▪ To demonstrate understanding of langugage content and concepts in planning
Mathematics teaching strategies;

▪ To develop lesson or activity plans that integrate language content and
concepts in Mathematics learning;

▪ To apply language integration in the design of Mathematics learning activities

▪ To raise the percentage of learners achieving at least the minimum level
of proficiency in numeracy standards
