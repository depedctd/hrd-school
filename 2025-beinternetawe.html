Be Internet Awesome 2025

Be Internet Awesome: A Digital Literacy and Safe Internet Use Training for Grades 4-6 Teachers

December 17, 18, 19, 2025

Venue: Gran Isabel, Constantino, Virac


Objectives

▪ identify and explain the five foundational lessons of the Be Internet Awesome curriculum
with 100% accuracy during group reporting or written assessment

▪ create the individual Workplace Application Plan on Be Internet Awesome and
submit the documents to SGOD-Human Resource Development

▪ demonstrate the application of newly acquired knowledge and skills by outlining
at least two concrete strategies to strengthen and protect their own online presence.


Target Participants:

Grades 4-6 Teachers teaching English, Filipino, ICT, Edukasyon sa Pagpapakatao, and Araling Panlipunan
