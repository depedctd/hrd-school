Division Training Workshop on Empowering Continuing Professional Development Facilitators on Effective Learning Facilitation

▪ To establish the structure, systems, and procedures for the institutionalization of SDO Catanduanes
CPD Qualified Learning Facilitators

▪ To select qualified CPD Learning Facilitators

▪ To enhance the Learning Facilitator's skills in facilitation and writing session guides


Target Participants:

Education Program Supervisors, Public School District Supervisors, selected SDO teaching-related personnel,
Secondary and Elementary School Heads, Teacher-in-Charge, Master Teacher, Key Teachers
