Bridging the Gap 2025

Division Training Workshop on Bridging the Gap: Streamling the Submission of Learning & Development (L&D)
in SDO and Schools Using the Document Tracking Platform

Victor Hall, VTC, Virac, Catanduanes


Objectives

▪ To update the approved Learning and Development activities conducted in SDO, schools,
and learning centers using the HRD Document Platform;

▪ To present emerging initiated L&D practices in SDO, schools, and learning centers;

▪ To give recognition to schools with emerging initiated L&D practices.
