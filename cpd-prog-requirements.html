Program Accreditation Requirements

Preparation

  1. CPDD-02 Application for Accreditation of CPD Program
  2. CPDD-14 Certificate for Participants/Attendees (A copy of what to be issued)
  3. Program of Activities showing time/duration of topics/workshop and resource persons
  4. CPDD-16 Instructional Design for Professional Teachers
  5. Evaluation method or tool that measures the learning gained by the participants specific and appropriate to course objectives set (Pre-Test/Post-Test. Daily Evaluation)
  6. CPDD-17 Resume of the Resource Person/s (including the required attachments)(Resource Persons must be a graduate of a Masteral Degree related to the topic)
  7. Valid Professional Identification Card/Receipt of Renewal of Resource Persons (Attach to Form “CPDD-17”) if licensed/registered professional. Otherwise, submit a copy of a valid government or company-issued Identification Card
  8. Financial Plan/Breakdown of Expenses (using the prescribed template pursuant to Commission Resolution No. 1520 S. 2022)
