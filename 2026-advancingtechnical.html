Advancing Technical Assistance

Training Workshop in Advancing Technical Assistance for Effective School Governance and Operations

January 26, 27, 28, 2026

Venue: Rhaj Executive Inn, Gogo, Virac


Objectives

▪ Apply with practical tools and strategies to conduct learnng needs assessments using validated data
with at least 90% accuracy in identifying L&D gaps.

▪ Developed contextualized learning and development plans using validated LNA results with at least
90% alignment to identify needs and priorities.

▪ Provide technical support to implementing learning and development plans for schools, using validated
assessment data with at least 90% compliance and alignment with L&D priorities.

▪ Submit District/School Professional Development Program for Quality Assurance and NEAP Certification
