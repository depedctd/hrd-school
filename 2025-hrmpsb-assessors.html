HRMPSB Assessors Training 2025

Human Resource Merit Promotion and Selection Board (HRMPSB) Assessors Training:
Deepening on Initial Evaluation and PPST Assessment at the Division Level

August 18-19, 2025
Rhaj Executive Inn, Virac, Catanduanes


Objectives

▪ Explain the key provisions of Executive No. 174, s. 2022 and the reclassification process;

▪ Evaluate qualification and performance requirement documents of applicants based on established
standards and indicators aligned with the Philippine Professional Standards for Teachers (PPST);

▪ Use the increment table, scoring rubric, and computation formula in evaluating and scoring Education, Training, Experience (ETE), and Performance

▪ Simulate the assessment process both Classroom Observable Indicators (COI) and Non-Observable Indicators (NCOI) using validated tools and protocols

▪ Apply the S.T.A.R approach in conducting BEI to ensure evidence-based and competency-focused evaluation.


Target Participants:

Identified Assessors from the Division Office and School
