Reminders:

  • The resource person must be a graduate of at least a Masteral degree for Level 6 qualification.
  • The resource person must have a training aligned to his/her assigned topic.
  • The number of pax, date, duration, venue must already be identified.
  • "Download"-required to use the PRC template; "View Sample"-use DepEd official template or if none, customize.
  • NEAP cascaded programs are already quality assured and certified like IPBT, MATATAG Program, ILT
  • If the training program is division or school-initiated, obtain a NEAP QA certification first before submitting docs for CPD program accreditation. Click here to see requirements

Steps:

  • Consolidate all accomplished and signed documents into one (1) PDF file.
  • Upload the consolidated PDF file, including the raw files, in your One Drive or Google Drive account.
  • Create a shortened link of your online folder to be shared to HRD Section.
  • Submit a physical copy with transmittal letter containing the link to HRD Section.
  • Wait for the SDS endorsement to be approved.
